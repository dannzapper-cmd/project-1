## What the playlist covers

- Landing and product positioning  
- `/demo` dashboard in replay-safe mode  
- Lead table, detail drawer, agent trace, and QA evaluation  
- Human review and local CSV export (no email sending, no CRM writes)

---

## Safety boundaries (unchanged in the video narrative)

- LeadForge **does not send email** or write to a CRM.  
- Public demo defaults to **replay mode**; live Groq is **backend-controlled**, rate-limited, and not unlimited on the public site.  
- Outreach copy is **draft-only** for human review.
